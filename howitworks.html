--- title: How it Works layout: layout ---
  • 1 Work for as long or short as you like, until you need a break.
  • 2
    Divide your recent work session by 3 (or your chosen fraction) to determine the break duration.
  • 3
    Take a break until the notification sounds or until you choose to return to work.
  • 4 Return to step 1.

Additional rules:
If you have to stop work for a non-work-related interruption, start a break immediately.
